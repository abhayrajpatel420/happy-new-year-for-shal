Why We Celebrate

The Constitution

The Constitution of India came into effect on January 26, 1950, replacing the Government of India Act (1935).

Purna Swaraj

It was on this day in 1930 when the Declaration of Indian Independence (Purna Swaraj) was proclaimed.

Rajpath Parade

The main celebration is held in New Delhi at the Kartavya Path with a grand parade showcasing India's power.
